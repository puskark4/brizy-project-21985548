Mission Statement

We are a group of technology enthusiasts, and this is our solution to fixing email scams and spam.


We decided to build this service primarily for people who do not necessarily have a technical background and may find it challenging to use sophisticated websites to detect email content. We intend for this service to be available to everyone in a seamless manner—and for free. To achieve this, we made it an email-based service, though we plan to expand to other avenues, Chrome extension and Mobile Applications being next.


Why is this completely free for individuals?

We believe everyone should have access to tools that help them stay safe online. By offering this service for free, we aim to reduce spam, scams, and phishing attempts, protecting individuals from the growing threats posed by cybercriminals. As malicious actors become more sophisticated—especially in the era of AI—we hope to play a role in safeguarding society from digital fraud.

Additionally, our enterprise plans help subsidize the costs of our free personal plans. If you wish to contribute, please connect us with businesses that might be interested in using our services for their enterprise needs.


Once I email you, will you use my data for training or otherwise?

No, any emails sent directly to our service mailbox are not used for any training or marketing.


What is your data security and sovereignty Policy?

We take your data privacy very seriously and will never use the data you send to us for any purpose—marketing or training—without your consent.

Data is sent to Google and OpenAI services for analysis, depending on use cases and you can review their respective websites to understand how they handle data.

For enhanced security, we also offer a Bring Your Own AI tier, where our experts will work with you to ensure that your data never leaves your tenancy by utilizing self-hosted and cloud AI models. For full review of our privacy policy, visit our privacy policy page.
